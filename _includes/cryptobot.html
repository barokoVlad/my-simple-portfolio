  • 20.05.23 - 03.08.23
Project Review

The project is a powerful and innovative tool designed to provide users with up-to-date and accurate information about the stock and cryptocurrency markets. This bot acts as a reliable source of news and analytics for investors, traders, and anyone interested in solving problematic issues related to the world of finance.


Based on the Telegram platform, this bot provides a continuous stream of information on various aspects of the financial market. One of the key features of the bot is notifications about the opening and closing of stock markets. Users will be able to receive updates on the mode of operation of the markets, which will allow them to better plan their transactions.


The bot also provides information about the opening and closing of candlesticks, which are important indicators of price dynamics. This will help traders track trends and make informed trading decisions. In addition, the bot provides data on financing rates and updates on them, which are important factors for those involved in lending and loan operations.


One of the main aspects that make this bot unique is the notification of large transactions in the market made by whales. This information helps traders understand the dynamics of price movements and respond to possible changes in the market in time.

As for news, the bot always provides its users with access to reliable sources of information. It automatically extracts news from trusted platforms such as Incrypted, ensuring that its users have access to reliable and up-to-date materials. In addition, the bot also provides the ability to subscribe to messages from a large list of Twitter accounts. These accounts are owned by coins, exchanges, high-profile individuals, and reputable analysts, making the information more diverse and relevant.


Thanks to the combination of these features, the Telegram bot project for sending information to clients becomes an indispensable tool for traders and investors, helping them make informed decisions based on up-to-date data and reliable information. The bot provides easy access to important information, which helps users to work effectively in the financial markets and achieve their financial goals.
